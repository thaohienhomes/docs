## 4 Artifact types

- **Interactive Image** — SVG infographics, illustrations
- **Generative Diagram** — Flowcharts, mindmaps, architecture diagrams
- **Content Visualizer** — Data tables, charts, dashboards
- **AI Rendering** — Runnable code, React components, interactive apps
