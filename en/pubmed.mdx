## How to search

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate PubMed skill">
    Click **Skills** (⚡) → find **PubMed Search** → activate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your query">
    Type your search in English or Vietnamese. Example: "Search PubMed for RCTs on metformin and diabetes in the last 5 years"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review results">
    AI returns articles with title, authors, abstract, PMID, and direct links.
  </Step>
</Steps>
