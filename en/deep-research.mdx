## How to use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate Deep Research">
    Click the **Deep Research** icon (🔍) or type "/deep-research" before your question.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask your research question">
    Be specific for better results. Example: "Effectiveness of CBT vs medication for adolescent depression: evidence from RCTs 2020-2025"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor progress">
    AI shows real-time progress: sources being searched, articles being read, information being extracted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the report">
    Results are presented as a structured report with full citations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
