<Note>Available on **Medical** plan and above.</Note>

## How to use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate the skill">
    Click **Skills** (⚡) → find **Drug Interaction Checker** → activate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter medications">
    List the drugs to check. Example: "Check interactions between Warfarin, Aspirin, Omeprazole, Metformin"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review results">
    AI returns interactions with severity level (Major/Moderate/Minor), mechanism, and recommendations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
