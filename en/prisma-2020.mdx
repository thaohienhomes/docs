## How to use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate PRISMA skill">
    Click **Skills** (⚡) → find **PRISMA 2020** → activate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your research question">
    Be specific. Example: "PRISMA 2020 systematic review: effectiveness of telemedicine for type 2 diabetes management"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the protocol">
    AI generates a protocol with PICO, search strategy, and inclusion/exclusion criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View screening results">
    AI searches PubMed and Semantic Scholar, screens titles/abstracts, and extracts data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Download the complete report with PRISMA flow diagram and checklist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
