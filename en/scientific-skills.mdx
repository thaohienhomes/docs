## How to use

Click **Skills** (⚡) → browse or search → activate. Skills enhance AI responses for specific research tasks.

## Main categories

- **Quality Assessment**: GRADE, RoB 2, ROBINS-I, Newcastle-Ottawa Scale
- **Study Design**: PICO, CONSORT, STROBE, CARE, STARD
- **Statistical Analysis**: Meta-analysis, sample size, statistical tests
- **Literature Search**: PubMed, Semantic Scholar, ClinicalTrials.gov, CrossRef
