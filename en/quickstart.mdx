## Get Started in 5 Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [pho.chat](https://pho.chat) and click **Sign Up**. Register with email or sign in with Google/GitHub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your field">
    Select your primary use case: Research, Education, or Medical. You can switch anytime.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick an AI model">
    Click the model selector at the top of the chat. We recommend **Gemini 2.0 Flash** to start — fast, free, and great with Vietnamese.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Type your question and press **Enter**. Try asking about your research topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Scientific Skills">
    Click the **Skills** icon (⚡) to browse 170+ research skills. Activate one to get specialized AI responses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
